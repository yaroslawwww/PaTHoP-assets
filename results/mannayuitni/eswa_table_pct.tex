\textbf{Note:} 
\textbf{$H$}: Forecasting horizon steps. 
\textbf{$B_{10k}, B_{30k}$}: Performance of the target series trained on the original limited sample ($10^4$ points) and the extended sample ($3 \times 10^4$ points), respectively. 
\textbf{Median}: The median metric value across the selected donor group. 
\textbf{Win (\%)}: Share of donors in the selected group that outperformed the \textit{weakest} baseline (values $< \max(B_{10k}, B_{30k})$). 
\textbf{AbsWin (\%) (Absolute Winner)}: Share of donors in the elite group that outperformed the \textit{strongest} baseline (values $< \min(B_{10k}, B_{30k})$), representing a quality gain unattainable by the target series alone. 
\textbf{Lose (\%)}: Share of donors performing worse than both baselines. 
\textbf{$D (\%)$ (Defect Rate)}: The proportion of donors resulting in negative transfer, defined as an error increase $> 1.5 \times B_{10k}$. 
\textbf{$p$-val}: Statistical significance of the improvement over random selection (Mann-Whitney U test, one-sided).
